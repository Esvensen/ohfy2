Sophie Mo is a London based multidisciplinary artist and tattooer, specialising in illustrative blackwork and taking inspiration from natural forms often infusing them with gothic and avant garde elements. Sophie is happy to work at any scale and works predominantly freehand. 

To book in with her please email - hello@sophiemo.com or book via her tattoodo - https://ta2.do/sophiemo
